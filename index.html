Crystal Collector

When the game begins, you'll be given a random number. You'll add a specific number of points to your score, which will be revealed after clicking on one of the four crystals below.

The game is won by matching your current score to the random number, but be careful not to let your score go above the random number.

The value of the crystals (and the random number) will reset after every win or loss.
